CampusCare

We are here to accompany you in your journey of a comfortable campus life! Here, you can report problematic facilities all around the campus area. You may also view whether your report has been taken care of.

Use our intuitive "Report" feature to upload photos and details of facility issues in seconds. Sort reports by location or recent-latest to stay informed. For more details, use Search bar. In the report system, you can only report one facility per area.

No more wondering if your report was received. Our dashboard displays statuses from Reported to In-Progress to Fixed, so you know exactly when help is on the way.

Browse our "Fixed Facilities" gallery to see the tangible improvements being made across campus every day.
